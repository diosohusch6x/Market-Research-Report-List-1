<p><p>High power reed switches, rated above 30W, are designed to control high voltages and currents in various applications, such as industrial machinery and automotive systems. These switches come in two primary market types: normally open (NO) and normally closed (NC). Normally open switches remain off until activated, making them ideal for applications where control is needed during active states. Conversely, normally closed switches are energized until a control signal is applied, suitable for safety and emergency systems requiring constant monitoring.</p></p>
