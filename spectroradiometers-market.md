<p><ul><li>Atomic Absorption Spectrometer</li><li>Molecular Absorption Spectrometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The spectroradiometer market features distinct types based on measurement principles. Atomic Absorption Spectrometers (AAS) analyze the absorption of light by free atoms to determine concentrations of metallic elements in samples, emphasizing precision in trace metal analysis. In contrast, Molecular Absorption Spectrometers measure the absorption of light by molecules, providing information on various chemical compounds within a sample. Both types are essential for applications across environmental monitoring, pharmaceuticals, food safety, and materials science, offering critical insights into chemical composition and concentration.</p></p>
