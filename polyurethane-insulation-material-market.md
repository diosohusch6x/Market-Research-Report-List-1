<p><p>The polyurethane insulation material market consists of three main types: flexible foam, rigid foam, and spray foam. Flexible foam is commonly used in cushioning and insulation applications due to its lightweight and compressible nature. Rigid foam offers superior thermal resistance and structural support, making it ideal for walls and roofs. Spray foam creates an airtight seal and provides high insulation efficiency, suitable for irregular spaces and gaps. Each type caters to specific insulation needs across residential, commercial, and industrial sectors.</p></p>
