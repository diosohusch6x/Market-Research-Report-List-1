<p><ul><li>Pneumatic</li><li>Solid</li><li>Polyurethane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dirt bike tire market is primarily divided into three types: pneumatic, solid, and polyurethane tires. Pneumatic tires are air-filled, offering superior cushioning and traction on uneven terrain, making them the most popular choice for off-road riding. Solid tires, made from rubber or similar materials, provide durability and low maintenance but lack the shock absorption of pneumatic options. Polyurethane tires combine the benefits of both, offering a lightweight, puncture-resistant solution with decent cushioning, suitable for various riding conditions.</p></p>
