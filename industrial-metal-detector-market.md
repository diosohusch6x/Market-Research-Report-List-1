<p><p>The industrial metal detector market encompasses various types tailored for specific applications. Rectangular aperture metal detectors are designed for flat surfaces and packaging, providing high sensitivity. Gravity fall metal detectors are utilized for bulk materials, detecting contaminants as they fall through a chute. Pipeline liquid, paste, and slurry metal detectors ensure product safety in fluid transport systems. Lastly, metal detectors with conveyor systems integrate seamlessly into production lines, enabling continuous inspection of products as they move, ensuring quality and safety in manufacturing.</p></p>
