<p><p>The packaging coating additives market includes several specialized types that enhance performance and functionality. Slip additives reduce friction between surfaces for smoother movement and easier peeling. Antistatic additives prevent static charge build-up, ensuring safe handling of sensitive materials. Anti-fog additives minimize moisture condensation, maintaining clarity in packaging. Anti-block additives prevent layers from sticking together, facilitating easier handling. Antimicrobial additives inhibit microbial growth, ensuring product safety and extending shelf life. Collectively, these additives improve packaging efficiency and product longevity.</p></p>
