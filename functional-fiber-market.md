<p><p>The functional fiber market encompasses several types based on distinct functions. Physical function relates to fibers that enhance structural integrity, improving durability and comfort. Chemical function fibers possess properties that can interact with the environment, such as moisture management or antimicrobial effects. Material separability function highlights fibers that allow for easy recycling or separation of materials in composite products. Finally, bioadaptive function fibers respond to external stimuli, adapting to conditions like temperature or humidity for enhanced performance and user comfort.</p></p>
