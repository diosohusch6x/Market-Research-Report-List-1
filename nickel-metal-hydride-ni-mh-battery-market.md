<p><p>Nickel-Metal Hydride (Ni-MH) batteries are rechargeable energy storage devices utilizing a nickel oxide hydroxide cathode and a hydrogen-absorbing alloy anode. Ni-MH batteries find extensive applications in consumer electronics, hybrid electric vehicles (HEVs), and renewable energy systems.</p><p>The Nickel-Metal Hydride (Ni-MH) Battery Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9.4% during the forecast period, driven by increasing demand for sustainable and efficient energy storage solutions.
