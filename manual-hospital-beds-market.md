<p><ul><li>Single Shake Bed</li><li>Double Shake Bed</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The manual hospital beds market includes various types designed for patient comfort and care. Single shake beds allow for basic adjustments, typically elevating the head or foot section, catering to individual patient needs. In contrast, double shake beds offer more versatile positioning options, enabling both the head and foot sections to be adjusted independently. These beds are crucial in hospitals for facilitating patient mobility, enhancing care delivery, and ensuring comfort during recovery, ultimately improving overall patient outcomes.</p></p>
