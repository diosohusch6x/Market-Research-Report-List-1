<p><p>The automotive batteries market comprises several types, primarily including VRLA (Valve-Regulated Lead Acid) batteries, flooded batteries, and others. VRLA batteries are sealed and maintenance-free, suitable for various vehicles and applications. Flooded batteries, on the other hand, contain liquid electrolyte and require regular maintenance, making them common in traditional vehicles. Other types may include lithium-ion and nickel-metal hydride batteries, which are increasingly used in electric and hybrid vehicles due to their lightweight and high energy density. Together, they cater to diverse automotive energy needs.</p></p>
