<p><strong>The Powder Caramel Color Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Plain Caramel Color</li><li>Ammonia Caramel Color</li><li>Ammonia Sulfite Caramel Color</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The powder caramel color market encompasses three main types: plain caramel color, ammonia caramel color, and ammonia sulfite caramel color. Plain caramel color is derived from heating sugars without additives, providing a simple coloring agent. Ammonia caramel color is produced using ammonia, resulting in a darker hue, often used in beverages. Ammonia sulfite caramel color includes sulfites, enhancing stability and improving color consistency, commonly utilized in sauces and baked goods. Each type serves distinct applications based on desired characteristics.</p></p>
