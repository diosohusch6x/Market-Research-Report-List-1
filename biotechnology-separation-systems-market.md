<p><p>The biotechnology separation systems market comprises various technologies used to isolate and purify biological molecules. Membrane filtration employs semi-permeable membranes to separate particles based on size. Liquid chromatography utilizes porous media to separate components based on interactions with stationary and mobile phases. Centrifuges separate substances via density differences by spinning samples at high speeds. Electrophoresis separates molecules based on their charge and size in an electric field. Flow cytometry analyzes and sorts cells using laser technology. Other methods include additional innovative separation techniques.</p></p>
