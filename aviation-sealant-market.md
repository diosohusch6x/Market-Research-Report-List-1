<p><p>The aviation sealant market is primarily divided into two types: organic and inorganic sealants. Organic sealants, typically made from polymer-based compounds, offer flexibility, adhesion, and resistance to environmental factors, making them suitable for various aircraft applications. In contrast, inorganic sealants, often based on silicate or metal oxides, are known for their durability and high-temperature resistance, making them ideal for extreme conditions. Both types serve crucial roles in ensuring the structural integrity and safety of aircraft.</p></p>
