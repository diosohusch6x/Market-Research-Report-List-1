<p><p>The small power transformer market, typically defined for voltages below 220 kV, is primarily divided into dry-type and oil-immersed transformers. Dry-type transformers utilize air for cooling and are preferred in indoor applications due to their safety and low maintenance. Oil-immersed transformers, on the other hand, use oil for cooling and insulation, making them suitable for outdoor use and high-load applications. Both types serve critical roles in energy distribution in residential, commercial, and industrial sectors.</p></p>
