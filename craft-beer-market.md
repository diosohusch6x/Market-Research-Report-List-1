<p><strong>The Craft Beer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ales</li><li>Lagers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The craft beer market is primarily divided into two main types: ales and lagers. Ales are fermented at warmer temperatures using top-fermenting yeast, resulting in robust flavors and fruity aromas. Common styles include pale ales, IPAs, and stouts. Conversely, lagers are fermented at cooler temperatures with bottom-fermenting yeast, leading to a cleaner, crisper taste. Popular lager styles include pilsners and bocks. Both segments cater to diverse consumer preferences, driving innovation and variety within the craft beer industry.</p></p>
