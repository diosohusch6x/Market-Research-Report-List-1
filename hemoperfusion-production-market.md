<p><p>The hemoperfusion production market is primarily categorized into three types: resin adsorption, activated carbon adsorption, and others. Resin adsorption utilizes synthetic resins to selectively remove toxins and metabolites from blood, offering high efficiency and specificity. Activated carbon adsorption employs porous carbon materials to adsorb a wide range of impurities, making it versatile and cost-effective. The "others" segment includes alternative materials and technologies like silica-based adsorbents, providing innovative solutions for various therapeutic applications in blood purification.</p></p>
