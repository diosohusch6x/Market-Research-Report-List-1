<p><ul><li>GPS Compasse</li><li>GNSS Compasse</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The satellite compass market primarily consists of two types: GPS compasses and GNSS compasses. GPS compasses utilize the Global Positioning System to determine geographic location and orientation, making them ideal for navigation in various applications. GNSS compasses, on the other hand, use signals from multiple satellite systems (like GLONASS, Galileo, and BeiDou) for enhanced accuracy and reliability. Both types play crucial roles in marine navigation, aviation, and geospatial applications, benefiting from cutting-edge technology for precise positioning and navigation.</p></p>
