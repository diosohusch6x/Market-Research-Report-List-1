<p><p>The polishing slurry market encompasses various types, primarily including alumina slurry, colloidal silica slurry, and ceria slurries. Alumina slurries are widely used for their effective polishing properties on hard substrates, making them ideal for semiconductor and optical applications. Colloidal silica slurries offer excellent finishing capabilities, particularly in the electronics industry, due to their fine particle size and chemical stability. Ceria slurries are favored for demanding polishing tasks, such as in glass and ceramics, thanks to their exceptional abrasion resistance and low defect generation.</p></p>
