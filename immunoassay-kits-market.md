<p><p>Immunoassay kits are essential tools for detecting and quantifying specific proteins or antibodies in various samples. The fluorometric market utilizes fluorescent labels, providing high sensitivity and allowing for real-time monitoring. Colorimetric assays, on the other hand, rely on a color change in the presence of the target analyte, making them visually interpretable and cost-effective. Both types cater to diverse applications in clinical diagnostics, research, and environmental monitoring, with their unique advantages influencing the selection based on specific needs.</p></p>
