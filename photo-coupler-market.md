<p><p>The phot coupler market is categorized into non-linear and linear optocouplers, each serving distinct applications. Non-linear optocouplers are primarily used for switching and signal transmission in digital circuits due to their ability to operate at varying input voltages. In contrast, linear optocouplers provide precise analog signal transmission, making them ideal for applications requiring high linearity in data communication and signal conditioning. Both types play crucial roles in ensuring electrical isolation and signal integrity in various electronic systems.</p></p>
