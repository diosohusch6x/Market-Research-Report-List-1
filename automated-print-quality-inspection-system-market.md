<p><ul><li>In-line Print Quality Inspection System</li><li>Off-line Print Quality Inspection System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automated Print Quality Inspection System market is divided into two primary types: in-line and off-line inspection systems. In-line print quality inspection systems operate in real-time during the printing process, allowing for immediate detection and correction of defects, enhancing workflow efficiency. In contrast, off-line print quality inspection systems analyze printed materials after the printing process, enabling thorough evaluations and detailed assessments, although they may introduce delays in production. Both types contribute to ensuring high print quality and consistency, catering to diverse industry needs.</p></p>
