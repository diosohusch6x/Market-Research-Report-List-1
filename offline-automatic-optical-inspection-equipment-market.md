<p><p>The Offline Automatic Optical Inspection (AOI) Equipment market primarily comprises two types: 2D and 3D AOI systems. </p><p>2D AOI Equipment captures flat images of the printed circuit boards (PCBs) to detect defects, focusing on surface issues and component alignment. In contrast, 3D AOI Equipment provides a volumetric view, enabling precise detection of height variations and solder joint integrity. Both types enhance quality control and boost manufacturing efficiency in electronics production, ensuring reliable and defect-free products before assembly.</p></p>
