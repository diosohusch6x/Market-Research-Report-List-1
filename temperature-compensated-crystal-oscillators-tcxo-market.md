<p><p>Temperature Compensated Crystal Oscillators (TCXOs) are precision timing devices that maintain frequency stability over varying temperature ranges. The market for TCXOs can be categorized into two primary shapes: SMD (Surface Mount Device) and PIN (Pin Type). SMD TCXOs are designed for compact, high-density applications and are solder-mounted directly onto circuit boards, while PIN TCXOs feature leads for through-hole mounting, providing robustness and ease of replacement. Each shape serves distinct applications, catering to different design requirements and space constraints in electronic systems.</p></p>
