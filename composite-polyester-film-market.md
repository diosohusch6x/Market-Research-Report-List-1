<p><strong>The Composite Polyester Film Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Biaxial Tension Type</li><li>Uniaxial Tension Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The composite polyester film market is primarily divided into two types: biaxial tension type and uniaxial tension type. Biaxial tension type films are stretched in both the machine and transverse directions, resulting in enhanced strength, flexibility, and optical clarity, making them ideal for packaging and electrical applications. In contrast, uniaxial tension type films are stretched in only one direction, providing specific properties suited for applications like labels and industrial uses, where directional strength is critical.</p></p>
